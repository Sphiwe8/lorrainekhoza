ABOUT US

Lorraine Khoza Foundation (LKF) is a dynamic non-profit organisation aimed at creating community based approaches addressing the root causes of Gender-Based Violence and inequalities. The organisation was founded out of the increasingly rising need for a dedicated and caring organisation for victims & survivors of gender based violence. In it’s various forms, abuse has cemented it’s oppressive presence in many of our lives because the voice against abuse & gender based violence needs to find it’s power.
Lorraine Khoza, the chairperson & founder is passionate about women and children. She founded the organization after she was sexually abused in her first year as a student. Having suffered the trauma and at the verge of giving up, she found her voice and has a vision to champion women’s rights and equality and to be a towering voice of strength for the marginalised. Despite the trauma she had experienced, she has now dedicated and committed her life to represent and advocate for the transformation and equality of women and children through the Lorraine Khoza Foundation.

VICTIM EMPOWERMENT AND SUPPORT SERVICES

  • Counselling

The day to day operations of the organizations are:
- Providing Psycho-social support through counselling for GBV victims and survivors
- Placing victims in shelters
- Assisting victims with reporting cases and all legal steps to be taken thereafter
- Monitoring and assessment of survivor's progress
- Victim empowerment
